<Section icon={Target} title="Combat System: Mastering the Triangle and Break Gauge">

The core of **dead or alive 6 version 1.30** remains the "Triangle System." This rock-paper-scissors mechanic ensures that no single playstyle is invincible. If an opponent is constantly blocking, you throw. If they are mashing strikes, you hold. If they are waiting to hold, you throw. Version 1.30 further polishes the Break Gauge, a meter that enables powerful defensive and offensive maneuvers.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Mechanic">
The Break Hold (50% Meter) is your only way to escape a Fatal Stun. Using it too early leaves you vulnerable to high-damage Break Blows later in the round.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Learn the Triangle System">
    Understand the hierarchy: Strikes beat Throws, Throws beat Holds, and Holds beat Strikes. Winning an interaction during a counter-hit state deals significantly more damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage the Break Gauge">
    The gauge fills as you deal and receive damage. At 50%, you can perform a **Break Hold** (Universal counter). At 100%, you can execute a **Break Blow**, a cinematic strike that parries incoming attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Practice Fatal Rush">
    By pressing the Special button repeatedly, you perform a **Fatal Rush**. The final hit causes a Fatal Stun, which can be transitioned into a Break Blow if your meter is full.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Side Attacks">
    Sidestepping is essential for avoiding linear strikes. Press the Special button while moving up or down to perform a **Side Attack**, punishing opponents who over-commit to straight-line combos.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Action | Input (PS/Xbox) | Meter Cost | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Break Blow** | Forward + S (R1/RB) | 100% | Powerful strike, parries high/mid |
| **Break Hold** | Back + S (R1/RB) | 50% | Universal hold, works on all heights |
| **Fatal Rush** | S, S, S, S | 0% | 4-hit combo, causes Fatal Stun |
| **Side Attack** | Up/Down + S | 0% | Evasive strike against linear moves |

</Section>
