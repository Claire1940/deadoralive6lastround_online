<Section icon={Target} title="Xbox Controls and Beginner Setup Guide">

Getting started with **dead or alive 6 xbox** is easier than most 3D fighters thanks to the dedicated "Special" button. This button allows beginners to perform Fatal Rushes and Break Blows without complex directional inputs. Below is the optimized control layout for the Xbox Wireless Controller.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Configure Your Controller">
    Go to Settings > Controls. Ensure that **Y** is set to Punch, **B** to Kick, **X** to Hold, and **A** to Throw. Map the **RB** button to the Special action for easy access to the Break Gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Triangle System">
    Understand the hierarchy: Strikes beat Throws, Throws beat Holds, and Holds beat Strikes. If you anticipate a strike, use a Hold (X + Direction). If the opponent is blocking/holding, use a Throw (A).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Learn Defensive Heights">
    Hold the **X** button while standing to block High and Mid strikes. Hold **X** while crouching to block Low strikes. Remember that Mid strikes are split into Mid-Punch and Mid-Kick for Holds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute a Fatal Rush">
    Tap the **RB** (Special) button repeatedly. This performs a four-hit combo that finishes with a powerful knockback. If your Break Gauge is full, the final hit automatically becomes a cinematic Break Blow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Input | Xbox Button | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **K** | **B** | Kick |
| **H** | **X** | Hold / Guard |
| **T** | **A** | Throw |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Movement Tip">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Break Gauge and Fatal Stun">

The combat in **dead or alive 6 xbox** revolves around the **Break Gauge**, a blue meter located under your health bar. Managing this resource is the difference between victory and defeat in high-level matches.

- **Break Blow (100% Gauge)**: A powerful strike that parries incoming attacks. Use it when you are backed into a corner or need to end a round with massive damage.
- **Break Hold (50% Gauge)**: A universal counter that works against any strike height (High, Mid, or Low). This is your primary escape tool when trapped in a **Critical Stun**.
- **Fatal Stun**: Triggered by the third hit of a Fatal Rush. During this stun, the opponent cannot perform normal holds, forcing them to use a Break Hold or take the full combo.

| Mechanic | Gauge Cost | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Break Blow** | 100% | Cinematic high-damage strike |
| **Break Hold** | 50% | Universal defensive counter |
| **Fatal Rush** | 0% | Simple 4-hit auto-combo |
| **Side Attack** | 0% | Evasive strike during sidestep |

<Callout type="warning" title="Gauge Management">
Do not waste your Break Gauge on a Break Blow if the opponent has enough health to survive. It is often better to save 50% for a **Break Hold** to prevent the opponent from finishing you with their own combo.
</Callout>

</Section>
