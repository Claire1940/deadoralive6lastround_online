Because she lacks a formal master, her moves are often slightly less polished versions of other characters' signatures, but she compensates with unpredictable timing and high damage potential. In **dead or alive 6 honoka** gameplay, understanding the "Triangle System" is the first step toward victory.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide: Basic Combo Routing">

To succeed with **dead or alive 6 honoka**, you must move beyond simple button mashing. The 2026 *Last Round* update maintains the core combo logic while rewarding precise execution of the Break Gauge systems. Follow these steps to build a reliable offensive foundation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize the Fatal Stun">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Execute the Fatal Rush">
    Press the Special (S) button repeatedly. This simple combo route is beginner-friendly and pushes the opponent back, building your meter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Confirm the Launcher">
    Once the opponent is stunned, use a launcher move to send them into the air. This is the moment where Honoka's damage potential spikes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Air Juggle and Ender">
    Connect with 2-3 mid-air hits. Finish the sequence with a knockback move to push the opponent into a **Danger Zone** for extra environmental damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Break Blow Finish">
    If your Break Gauge is at 100%, end your Fatal Rush with a final S press to trigger the Break Blow, dealing massive cinematic damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>
