<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Triangle Combat System">

The core of **Dead or Alive 6 PS4** is the "Triangle System." This is a sophisticated rock-paper-scissors mechanic that dictates every interaction in a match. Mastering this system is more important than memorizing long combo strings, as a single well-timed "Hold" can reverse the momentum of a fight.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Strikes Beat Throws">
    Attacks like punches and kicks will interrupt an opponent attempting a throw. If you see an opponent getting too close for a grapple, use a quick jab to reset the neutral game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Throws Beat Holds">
    If an opponent is constantly using defensive "Holds" (counters), they are vulnerable to throws. Throws against a holding opponent deal "Hi Counter" damage, significantly draining their health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Holds Beat Strikes">
    Holds are the signature counter-moves of the series. By matching the height of an incoming strike (High, Mid-Punch, Mid-Kick, or Low), you can catch the limb and perform a powerful reversal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Beyond the triangle, the **Break Gauge** adds a layer of resource management. This meter allows for two critical actions:
- **Break Blow**: A powerful, cinematic strike that parries incoming attacks and deals massive damage.
- **Break Hold**: A universal counter that works against any strike height, costing 50% of your meter. It is your primary "Get Out of Jail Free" card when trapped in a combo.

<Callout type="tip" title="Beginner Strategy">
Focus on using the **Fatal Rush** (mapped to the Special button). By pressing it four times, your character performs a simple, effective combo. If your Break Gauge is full, the final hit automatically becomes a devastating Break Blow.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="DLC, Costumes, and Save Transfers">

One of the most complex aspects of **Dead or Alive 6 PS4** is its DLC structure. With multiple Season Passes and hundreds of individual costumes, it is easy to get overwhelmed. The 2026 Last Round update simplifies this by including many former DLC packs in the base game.

**Transferring Data from PS4 to PS5:**
If you are moving from the original PS4 version to the Last Round update on PS5, your progress can carry over provided you use the same PlayStation Network account.

| Transferable Item | Status | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Save Data** | Supported | Includes story and quest progress |
| **Costumes** | Supported | Most purchased DLC transfers over |
| **Premium Tickets** | Not Supported | Spend these on PS4 before moving |
| **Rankings** | Not Supported | Online ranks are reset for Last Round |
| **KOF Characters** | Supported | Mai and Kula transfer if owned |

<Callout type="info" title="DLC Warning">
