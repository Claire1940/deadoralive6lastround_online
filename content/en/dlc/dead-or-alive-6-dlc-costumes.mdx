<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Costumes in DOA Central">

Not every outfit requires a credit card. A significant portion of the **dead or alive 6 dlc costumes** can be unlocked through gameplay by earning Owner Points and Costume Patterns. In the 2026 Last Round version, the process has been streamlined to reduce the "grind" seen in the original launch.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Earn Costume Patterns">
    Play through **DOA Quest** or **Arcade Mode**. Patterns are awarded upon completion. In Last Round, patterns are no longer randomized; they apply directly to the character you are playing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Accumulate Owner Points">
    Owner Points are the primary in-game currency. You earn these by participating in Online Ranked Matches, Lobby Matches, and clearing Training missions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Visit the Wardrobe">
    Navigate to **DOA Central > Wardrobe**. Select your character and find the "Locked" costume. If you have enough patterns, you can spend Owner Points to permanently unlock the outfit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Customizations">
    Once unlocked, you can further customize costumes with different hairstyles and accessories (glasses, etc.) using additional Owner Points.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Last Round Update">
The 2026 update ensures that all 29 standard characters have their base "Costume 01" through "Costume 05" unlocked by default, saving players thousands of Owner Points.
</Callout>

</Section>
