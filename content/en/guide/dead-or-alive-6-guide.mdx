<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat System and The Triangle Mechanic">

At the heart of any **dead or alive 6 guide** is the "Triangle System." This rock-paper-scissors mechanic dictates every interaction in the arena. Mastering this system allows you to punish "mashers" and predict high-level opponents.

| Action | Beats | Loses To | Result of Winning |
| | | | |
| **Strikes (P/K)** | Throws | Holds | High-speed damage, Stuns |
| **Throws (T)** | Holds | Strikes | Unblockable damage, Position swap |
| **Holds (H)** | Strikes | Throws | Counter-damage, Pressure reset |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Stun">
When you are in Critical Stun, you cannot block or throw, but you **can** still perform Holds. High-level players will bait your hold to land a high-damage throw, so vary your timing.
</Callout>

### Break Gauge Systems

The Break Gauge is a meter that fills as you deal and receive damage. It allows for two powerful maneuvers:

1.  **Break Blow (100% Meter)**: A powerful strike that parries incoming attacks and deals massive damage. It can be "cancelled" to extend combos.
2.  **Break Hold (50% Meter)**: A universal hold that works against high, mid, and low strikes simultaneously. It is your ultimate "get out of jail free" card when trapped in a combo.
