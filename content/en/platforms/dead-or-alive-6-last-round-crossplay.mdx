  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upload Saved Data">
    Launch the original game, navigate to the Main Menu, and select the option to upload your DLC and save data to the cloud (PSN, Xbox Network, or Steam Cloud).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prepare the Target Console">
    On your new console (e.g., PS5), ensure you are signed into the **exact same account** used for the original purchase. Data cannot be transferred between different user IDs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Initialize Last Round">
    Start Dead Or Alive 6 Last Round. Upon reaching the title screen, the game will automatically detect the cloud data and prompt you with a "Transfer" message.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Verify Content">
    Check the "Closet" and "Story Mode" to confirm your costumes and progress have successfully migrated. Note that some items like Ranking Points will reset.
  </Step>
</Steps>
