<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Controls and System Mechanics">

Success in this 3D arena requires mastering the fundamental control layout and understanding the underlying combat systems. The game relies on a unique defensive and offensive flow that sets it apart from traditional 2D fighters [3]. Follow these steps to build a solid foundation before taking your fights online [3].

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Memorize the Default Button Layout">
    Familiarize yourself with the five core inputs: Punch, Kick, Hold, Throw, and Special [3].
    
    - **PlayStation**: Triangle (Punch), Circle (Kick), Square (Hold), Cross (Throw), R1 (Special) [3].
    - **Xbox**: Y (Punch), B (Kick), X (Hold), A (Throw), RB (Special) [3].
    - **Steam Keyboard**: K (Punch), L (Kick), J (Hold), M (Throw), I (Special) [3].
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Triangle System">
    Internalize the core rock-paper-scissors rule: **Strikes beat Throws**, **Throws beat Holds**, and **Holds beat Strikes** [3]. A successful interaction on a hard read deals massive counter-damage [3].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize the Special Button">
    Use the Special button to execute crucial defensive maneuvers: sidestepping to dodge linear attacks, executing Fatal Rush combos, or triggering high-damage Break Blows [3].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage the Break Gauge">
    Monitor your meter closely. At 50% gauge, you can perform a universal **Break Hold** to escape any stun [3]. At 100% gauge, you can unleash a crushing **Break Blow** that armor-strikes through enemy attacks [3].
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Defensive Tip">
Do not mash buttons when trapped in a stun sequence. Instead, save 50% of your Break Gauge to execute a **Break Hold**. This universal counter works against high, mid, and low strikes, instantly resetting the neutral game [3].
</Callout>
