<Section icon={Swords} title="Essential Moves & Triangle System Strategy">

The combat system in *Dead or Alive 6* is governed by the Triangle System: strikes beat throws, throws beat holds, and holds beat strikes [1.4]. Mastering this system with Kasumi requires a balanced approach. Because her strikes are so fast, opponents will frequently attempt to use defensive holds to interrupt her pressure [1.4]. This is when you must transition from striking to utilizing her high-damage command throws.

Her move list is expansive, but several key moves form the foundation of her competitive gameplay. Knowing the frame data and hit properties of these tools will help you maintain control of the arena.

| Command | Hit Type | Startup Frames | Damage | Properties |
| P, P, P | High, High, High | 9 frames | 30 | Fastest jab string to check pressure [1.4] |
| 6P | Mid Punch | 11 frames | 19 | Excellent counter-poke and stun starter [1.4] |
| 4K | Mid Kick | 14 frames | 22 | Safe on block, pushes opponent back [1.4] |
| 236T | Throw | 5 frames | 55 | High-damage command throw against holds [1.4] |

When pressuring an opponent, do not complete her basic strings every time. Stopping a string midway leaves you at a frame advantage and allows you to execute a command throw if the opponent attempts to hold. If they remain defensive and block, utilize low-hitting sweeps to break their guard.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Predictable Teleports">
While Kasumi's teleports look spectacular, they have distinct audio cues and visual tells. Relying on them too heavily will result in opponents easily blocking and executing high-damage punishes. Use them sparingly to maintain the element of surprise.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Combo Guide & Routing">

To maximize your damage output with Kasumi, you must understand how to transition from basic ground pressure into high-flying air juggles. Her combo structure is logical and rewards precise timing. Follow this step-by-step progression to learn how to open up an opponent, launch them into the air, and execute a high-damage ender.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Critical Stun">
    Begin your offense with fast mid-hitting pokes like **6P** or **3P**. Landing these strikes on a counter-hit will place the opponent into a Critical Stun state, preventing them from blocking further attacks [1.4].
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Stun Extension">
    Once the opponent is stunned, extend the stun duration with a quick follow-up strike such as **4P**. This keeps them vulnerable and prevents them from recovering their guard [1.4].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Launcher">
    With the opponent deeply stunned, use a dedicated launching move like **8K** or **7K** to send them high into the air [1.4].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform the Air Juggle">
    While the opponent is airborne, execute a fast air-strike string. The standard juggle string is **P, P, P, P**, which keeps them suspended for maximum damage [1.4].
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Apply the Knockback Ender">
    Finish the combo with a high-impact knockback move such as **6P+K** to send them flying into a nearby wall or a stage hazard for extra environmental damage [1.4].
  </Step>
</Steps>

By practicing these steps, you can adapt your combos depending on your position on the stage. If you are near a hazard, always prioritize enders that push the opponent horizontally [1.4].
