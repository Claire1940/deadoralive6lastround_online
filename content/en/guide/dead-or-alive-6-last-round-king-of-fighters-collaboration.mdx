<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Purchase and Unlock KOF Characters">

To add these fighters to your select screen, follow the precise steps outlined below.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visit Your Platform's Store">
    Navigate to the PlayStation Store, Xbox Games Store, or Steam depending on your hardware. Ensure you have updated your game client to the latest version of *Dead or Alive 6 Last Round*.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the KOF Collaboration DLC">
    Search for the guest character keys individually or look for the bundled *King of Fighters XIV Collaboration Set*. This bundle includes both character unlock keys and their associated costume packs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase and Install">
    Complete your transaction. The platform storefront will automatically trigger a small license download. Restart your game client to apply the changes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify In-Game Roster">
    Launch the game and check the bottom of the character select screen. Mai Shiranui and Kula Diamond should now be fully unlocked for all offline and online game modes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Map} title="LOST PARADISE 'OBORO' Stage Features">

Alongside the guest fighters, the collaboration introduces a visually stunning remake of a fan-favorite arena: **Lost Paradise "Oboro"**. This stage utilizes Team NINJA's upgraded lighting system built directly into the KATANA ENGINE™. The OBORO system introduces realistic ambient light, soft natural shadows, and dynamic time-of-day changes that make the environmental hazards pop like never before.

The stage is divided into two distinct zones, each featuring unique environmental hazards that can turn the tide of battle when combined with high-damage combos.

| Hazard Zone | Trigger Condition | Damage Modifier | Strategic Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **T-Rex Nest** | Knockback near the dinosaur head | High | Triggers a cinematic attack, dealing massive unscalable damage |
| **Cliffside Drop** | Wall splat near the warning barrier | Medium | Forces a stage transition, resetting neutral with a positioning advantage |
| **Electric Fence** | Knockback into the perimeter wires | Medium | Stuns the opponent briefly, allowing for easy follow-up juggle combos |

Using these hazards effectively is key to maximizing your damage output, especially when playing characters with high knockback properties like Kula Diamond.

<Callout type="info" title="Oboro Lighting System">
The new OBORO lighting system is currently exclusive to the Lost Paradise stage at launch. Team NINJA has confirmed that free OBORO lighting updates for standard stages will be rolled out gradually in future game patches.
</Callout>
