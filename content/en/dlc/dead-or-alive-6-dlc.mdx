<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock and Manage DLC Content">

Managing your **dead or alive 6 dlc** requires using the in-game "DOA Central" hub. This is where you verify your purchases and apply them to your characters in the Wardrobe.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Purchase and Install">
    Navigate to the PlayStation Store, Xbox Store, or Steam. Purchase your desired Character Unlock Keys or Season Passes. Ensure the content is fully downloaded and installed before launching the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verify via DOA Central">
    Launch the game and enter **DOA Central**. Select **Wardrobe**. If you purchased a Character Unlock Key, that fighter should no longer have a "lock" icon on the selection screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock with Owner Points">
    Some costumes included in DLC packs still require "Owner Points" to be spent in the Wardrobe to fully activate them for use. These points are earned by playing DOA Quest, Arcade, or Online modes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Premium Tickets (PS4 Only)">
    If you are still on the original PS4 version, you can use Premium Tickets to unlock individual costumes. Note that these tickets **do not** transfer to the Last Round version on PS5.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="DOA Quest Rewards">
