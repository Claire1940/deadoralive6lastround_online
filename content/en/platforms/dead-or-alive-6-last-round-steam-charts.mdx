<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Install Proton Up-Qt">
    Switch to Desktop Mode on your Steam Deck and download **Proton Up-Qt** from the Discover Store. This tool allows you to easily manage custom compatibility layers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Download Proton GE">
    Open the app and install the latest version of **Proton GE** (e.g., version 11-1 or newer). This is crucial for fixing video codec issues that cause black screens in story mode.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Force Compatibility">
    Return to Gaming Mode, go to the game's properties, select the **Compatibility** tab, and check "Force the use of a specific Steam Play compatibility tool." Select the Proton GE version you just installed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Configure In-Game Graphics">
    Launch the game and ensure **Obero Mode** is disabled. Even on high-end portable PCs, this mode is not optimized for the game's engine and will cause significant stuttering.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Cutscene Bug">
Without Proton GE, the game will play perfectly during fights but will fail to render any cinematic content. If you plan on playing the Story Mode, this setup is mandatory.
</Callout>
