<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Combat Mechanics">

Success in Dead or Alive 6 depends on mastering the Triangle System. This rock-paper-scissors mechanic is the heart of the game's defensive and offensive flow.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Learn the Triangle">
    Strikes beat Throws, Throws beat Holds, and Holds beat Strikes. Always observe your opponent's tendencies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Break Gauge Usage">
    Fill your gauge to 50% for a **Break Hold** (universal counter) or 100% for a **Break Blow** (massive damage).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fatal Rush Initiation">
    Use the Special button to trigger **Fatal Rush**, an accessible combo string for beginners to learn timing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Danger Zone Awareness">
    Position your opponent against walls or stage hazards to maximize combo damage and reset neutral flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Practice Advice">
Use the **Command Training** and **Combo Challenge** modes to practice these mechanics. Consistent repetition is the only way to build muscle memory for complex hold inputs.
</Callout>
