<Section icon={Swords} title="Beginner Combat Mechanics and Triangle System">

The core combat engine is built around a dynamic "triangle" system where strikes, throws, and holds interact in a rock-paper-scissors format. Understanding this relationship is critical to surviving online ranked matches.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Mashing Attacks">
Mashing strikes is easily punished by defensive holds. If your opponent recognizes a repetitive attack string, they can execute a mid-combo hold to reverse the momentum and deal massive counter-damage.
</Callout>

| Control Type | PlayStation Layout | Xbox Layout | Steam Keyboard Default |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Punch (P)** | Triangle | Y | K |
| **Kick (K)** | Circle | B | L |
| **Hold (H)** | Square | X | J |
| **Throw (T)** | Cross | A | M |
| **Special (S)** | R1 | RB | I |

To build a solid foundation, follow this progressive training route:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Triangle System">
    Understand that strikes beat throws, throws beat holds, and holds beat strikes. Winning an interaction via a counter-read deals up to 160% damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Learn High, Mid, and Low Defense">
    Standing guard blocks high and mid strikes but leaves you open to low sweeps and throws. Crouching guard blocks low strikes but fails against mid-level attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize the Special Button">
    The Special button (S) executes sidesteps, Side Attacks, and the Fatal Rush combo. It is the easiest way for beginners to trigger defensive escapes and simple combos.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage the Break Gauge">
    At 50% gauge, execute a Break Hold to counter any strike direction. At 100% gauge, unleash a Break Blow to strike through incoming attacks for massive damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
