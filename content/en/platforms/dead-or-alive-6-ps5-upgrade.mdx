<Section icon={Shield} title="Executing the Dead or Alive 6 PS5 Upgrade">

Migrating your profile and executing the **dead or alive 6 ps5 upgrade** requires a specific sequence to ensure your save data, unlocked costumes, and offline progress transfer correctly. Because the PS5 version is a native application, it cannot automatically read PS4 save data unless it is placed in the correct system directory.

To ensure a seamless transition, follow these structured migration steps before launching the game on your PlayStation 5.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your PS4 Save Data">
    Boot up your PlayStation 4 or access your PS4 storage on the PS5. Upload your latest *Dead or Alive 6* save data to the PlayStation Plus Cloud Storage, or copy it directly to a FAT32/exFAT formatted USB storage drive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Download the PS5 Save File">
    On your PlayStation 5, navigate to Settings > Saved Data and Game/App Settings > Saved Data (PS4). Download your save file from the Cloud Storage or copy it from your USB drive directly to the console's local SSD.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Install Dead or Alive 6 Last Round">
    Go to the PlayStation Store, search for *Dead or Alive 6 Last Round*, and install the native PS5 version. Ensure you are logged into the exact same PlayStation Network (PSN) account used to purchase the original game and DLCs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Initialize the Data Import">
    Launch the native PS5 version. The game will automatically scan your local storage for existing PS4 save files. Follow the on-screen prompts to import your progression, story mode completion, and unlocked database records.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="One-Way Transfer Notice">
Save data migration is strictly a one-way process. Once your progression is imported into the native PS5 version of Last Round, any new progress made on your PS5 profile cannot be transferred back to the original PS4 version.
</Callout>
