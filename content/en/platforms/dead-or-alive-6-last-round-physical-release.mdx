<Section icon={Target} title="Transferring DLC Costumes">

Transferring your collection of costumes requires an active network connection. You do not need to manually install the previous DLC files; the system will recognize your ownership via your account history on the PlayStation Network, XBOX Network, or Steam.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Update Original Game">
    Ensure your base game version is updated to 1.23 to enable data upload functionality.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upload DLC Data">
    Sign in to your account, launch the original game, and navigate to the main menu to trigger the upload process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initialize New Version">
    Launch the new game version using the same account. The system will automatically detect and link your previously purchased content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Requirement">
