<Section icon={Swords} title="Platform-Specific Save Migration Methods">

Each gaming platform utilizes a distinct architecture for handling cloud saves and local directory storage. Understanding the nuances of your specific console or PC environment is essential to prevent data corruption or lost progress during the migration process.

For PlayStation users, cloud storage via PlayStation Plus is the most reliable method, though physical USB drives remain a viable option for legacy hardware transfers. Xbox users benefit from a fully automated cloud infrastructure that synchronizes data silently in the background. PC players on Steam have the added flexibility of manual file manipulation, allowing them to copy raw save directories directly.

| Platform | Cloud Method | USB Method | Recommended Option |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| PS5 (Same Console) | Auto-detects local save | Not applicable | Local auto-detection |
| PS5 (New Console) | PlayStation Plus Cloud | Not supported for PS5 to PS5 | PlayStation Plus Cloud |
| Xbox Series X/S | Xbox Network Auto-sync | Not supported | Xbox Network Cloud |
| Steam (PC) | Steam Cloud Sync | Manual folder copy to KoeiTecmo path | Steam Cloud Sync |

To manually transfer your files on PC, navigate to the local directory where your save data is stored. Copy the entire folder located at:
`\Users\(Your Username)\Documents\KoeiTecmo\DEADORALIVE6`
Paste this folder into the identical directory path on your new PC before launching the upgraded edition. Ensure Steam Cloud is enabled in your Steam client settings to prevent the platform from overwriting your manual files with an empty cloud save.

According to the [official Team NINJA product documentation](https://teamninja-studio.com/doa6/lastround/us/productinfo/), cross-play and cross-platform save transfers are not supported. You cannot move save files or purchased DLC between PlayStation, Xbox, and Steam.

<Callout type="info" title="Network Connection Required">
An active, stable connection to your platform network (PlayStation Network, Xbox Network, or Steam) is mandatory throughout the entire transfer process. Offline profile verification is not supported, and attempting to transfer files while offline may result in corrupted save states.
</Callout>
