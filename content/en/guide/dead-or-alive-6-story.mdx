The grid is divided into horizontal lanes representing different character perspectives, while vertical columns represent the chronological flow of time. To progress, players must complete fights that range from simple sparring matches to high-stakes battles against elite opponents. Some nodes will remain locked until you complete specific character episodes, encouraging players to experience the story from multiple viewpoints. This structure ensures that you understand the motivations of minor characters before they intersect with the main plotline in the final chapters.

To navigate this system efficiently, players should follow a structured approach to unlocking the full narrative. Some chapters require you to complete fights with characters you might not be familiar with, making it essential to practice basic combos in training mode beforehand.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Key Plot Twists and Lore Analysis">

The lore of the series reaches a climax with several major revelations regarding the Mugen Tenshin bloodline and M.I.S.T.'s technological advancements. The discovery of Honoka's connection to Raidou explains her extraordinary ability to mimic advanced martial arts techniques instantly. This revelation creates a complex dynamic between Honoka, Ayane, and Kasumi, as they realize they are bound by blood to one of the most dangerous entities in the franchise's history. Furthermore, the introduction of NiCO, a brilliant young scientist working for M.I.S.T., introduces cybernetic elements to the cloning process. By utilizing Honoka's latent energy, NiCO is able to stabilize the Phase 4 clones and initiate the resurrection of Raidou, transforming him into a cybernetic weapon designed to destroy the Mugen Tenshin clan once and for all.

Rig, who believed he was a simple refinery worker, is revealed to have been under mind control, serving as a pawn for Donovan's ultimate plans. This psychological manipulation highlights the lengths to which M.I.S.T. will go to secure their assets.
