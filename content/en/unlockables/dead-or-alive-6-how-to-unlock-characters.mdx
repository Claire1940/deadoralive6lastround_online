<Section icon={Target} title="dead or alive 6 how to unlock characters: Step-by-Step Guide">

If you are playing the free-to-play Core Fighters edition, you will find the majority of the roster greyed out on the selection screen. Knowing **dead or alive 6 how to unlock characters** is essential to expanding your roster and finding your preferred fighting style. 

Unlocking characters in this game is not tied to in-game achievements, Story Mode progression, or DOA Quest completions. Instead, character unlocks are managed through digital licenses purchased via your platform's store. Follow this step-by-step process to unlock your desired fighters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Your Current Game Version">
    Check whether you are playing the original Core Fighters or the 2026 Last Round Core Fighters. The original F2P version provides Kasumi, Hitomi, Diego, and Bass. The Last Round F2P version provides Kasumi, Marie Rose, Honoka, and NiCO.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Your Platform Store">
    Open the PlayStation Store, Xbox Store, or Steam depending on your console or PC setup. Search for the game's official add-on page to view all available downloadable content.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Your Unlock Method">
    Decide whether to purchase individual Character Unlock Keys or character bundles. Bundles like the "20 Characters Set" or "Female Fighters Set" offer a much lower cost-per-character compared to buying licenses individually.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchase and Install the License">
    Complete the transaction using your platform account. The store will download a small license file (usually a few kilobytes) that tells the game client to unlock the selected characters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Verify the Unlock In-Game">
    Launch the game. Go to Free Training or Versus Mode to confirm that the greyed-out character portraits are now fully colored and selectable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Try Before You Buy">
You do not need to buy a character's unlock key to play their Tutorial, Command Training, or Combo Challenge. Use these free training modes to test a character's moveset, speed, and difficulty rating before spending money on their license.
</Callout>

Purchasing character licenses is a permanent account-wide unlock. However, ensure that you purchase DLC from the same regional store as your base game, as cross-region DLC mismatch will prevent the characters from unlocking.
